RevivalKids


Empowering the Next Generation of Worshipers

We believe that our children are capable of carrying the mantle and have the potential to achieve great things. As dreamers, they have a unique connection to heaven and are able to believe in the impossible. Our goal is to provide them with the necessary tools and platform to excel and grow.

 

We are dedicated to investing our time and resources in our children and helping them develop not only their dance skills, but also their character.

 

 Our program is designed to encourage children to develop a love for God, themselves, and dance. Through a combination of fun and professional training, we aim to equip your child with the skills needed to become a serious dancer.

House of T’heArts Orlando is pleased to offer a variety of classes for young children, with the aim of fostering a love for God and the

performing arts from an early age.

 

Our children's classes cover a range of styles, including Praise Dance with instruments and Hip Hop.

 

In addition, we offer classes that help children grow spiritually and develop as worshipers, with a biblical-based curriculum that teaches children aged 6 and up to hear God's voice and understand

their identity and purpose in Christ.

 

No prior experience is necessary
