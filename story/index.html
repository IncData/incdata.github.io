Our origins

 

In 2009, Edward and Delki Rosso, two devoted Children of God, founded House of TheArts International School and Ministry in New York City. Delki, who had previously served as a dance leader in her local church, felt a strong calling from the Lord to establish a dance school. Together, they birthed a vision to train and equip men and women to become messengers of the Kingdom of God through the Arts. Today, House of T'heArts International School and Ministry continues to fulfill this mission with zeal and passion.

 

 

 

Following their success with around 100 students in the New York metropolitan area, the Lord appointed Edward and Delki to relocate their school to Orlando, FL.  Their new assigment was to establish their school and impact the community of Central Florida for Christ. With obedience to God's call, Edward and Delki bravely uprooted their lives and ministry to fulfill this divine assignment. Today, House of TheArts International School and Ministry continues to flourish in Orlando, impacting countless lives with the power of the Arts and the Gospel.  In March 2020, HOTAI celebrated the grand opening of their local school in the City of Orlando, marking a significant milestone in their journey.

 

House of T'heArts is dedicated to raising up and equipping people to use their gifts

to serve the Kingdom of God.

ROYALTY DESIGNS BOUTIQUE

 

In 2011, we answered God's call to establish House of the Arts International School & Ministry in New York. As our dance school flourished, we recognized the need for quality instruments and clothing. This led to the birth of Royalty Designs Boutique, guided by a simple rule: 'NEVER SELL ANYTHING I WOULD NOT WEAR MYSELF!' Our mission is to offer elegant and casual dancewear that embodies the Kingdom of God, adorning majesty and reflecting our divine royalty, all in praise of our Lord Jesus
