Directors

Edward & Delki Rosso

Edward & Delki Rosso are the founders and directors of House of T'heArts International and their vision is to awaken revival through the creative arts and prophetic worship. They have a beautiful family of three children (Dejoy, Juliette, and David). Delki is the founder and designer of Royalty Designs Boutique.


Both are speakers and lead our RevivalCamp conferences with the purpose to equip and train a new generation of creatives and worshipers both physically and spiritually to reach a higher level of intimacy with the Holy Spirit.


Our school is fortunate to have a dedicated core team and volunteers who are committed to realizing our vision and mission. Their unwavering passion, resilience, and love for our students and ministry are truly remarkable.
