VISION

AWAKENING REVIVAL THROUGH CREATIVE & PROPHETIC ARTS IN MY CITY AND THE NATIONS.

 

 ISAIAH 61:1-7

MISSION

We are dedicated to the restoration, empowerment, training, equipping, activation, and guidance of a generation of creative and prophetic worshipers, enabling individuals to nurture their God-given talents, unearth their prophetic calling, and walk in His divine purpose.

CORE VALUES



We love the Word of God

We abide in Him as He abides in us through the revelation of His word.



We seek unity

Just as Christ is one with the Father, so we are one with His body.



We are more than just dance

We build with people who honor His presence and honor each other. We are family!



Atmosphere Shifters

Children activated by the outpouring of the Holy Spirit to walk on Earth as they were originally designed in Heaven.

At House of T'heArts International School, we purposefully create an environment where God is our honored guest, inviting us to gather and find joy at His table, as described in Song of Solomon 2:4. We serve as the place for meaningful encounters and transformation within His community, paving the way for the Lord's glorious return.
