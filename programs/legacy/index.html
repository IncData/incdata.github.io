About the team

The Legacy Pre-Professional Dance Team Program is designed for dancers between the ages of 13 to 25 years old who are interested in becoming a pre-professional dancer or teacher in the near future.


Our classes are intentionally preparing the dancer for pre-professional training through classes in Ballet, modern dance, choreography and other techniques at different levels.


Auditions

Program & Auditions

At HOTAI Legacy, young dancers train in a positive and professional environment. Some of the participants will be selected to eventually join rehearsals alongside HOTAI Orlando Ministry and even House of T’heArts International Ministry for select ministry opportunities in and out of the country.

 

You can join this program by audition.  Our audition dates are displayed from time to time on the event page or in our agenda.

 

If you would like to schedule a casting outside of those dates, please email us at admin@houseofthearts.com
